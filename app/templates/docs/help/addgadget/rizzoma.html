Add a gadget to a wave in Rizzoma

  1. Move your cursor to where you want to insert the gadget.

  2. While holding Ctrl+Shift, click the “Gadgets” button.

  3. In the pop-up that appears, click in the text box labeled, “URL”. In that text box, paste {% if gadgetURL %}“{{ gadgetURL }}”{% else %}the gadget URL{% endif %}.
